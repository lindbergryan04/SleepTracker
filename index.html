What does it mean to sleep well?

Sleep is a complex process that involves the brain, hormones, and the body. It's not just about the amount of time you spend in bed, but also about the quality of your sleep. Sleep quality can be measured by many factors, including total sleep time, sleep fragmentation, wake after sleep onset, and more.
